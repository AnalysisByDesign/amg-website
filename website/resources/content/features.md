
**Analyse my Golf** is a crowd-sourced golf analysis system to help you record, track and improve your golf game. Enter each round of golf you play into our easy to use 'Stroke Tracker' on your laptop, tablet or mobile device either during or after your round, and let us analyse your game.

As a golfer, you want to see some improvement in your game after spending all those hours down the range and on the course! We can show you those improvements, and give you an indication of where those lost shots are going.

And what's best, for up to three rounds per month, all this is currently free for new memberships. This will increase to just &pound;10 for lifetime membership before long, so sign up now! Ideal for the casual golfer who can only play at the weekends.

##For the individual golfer ##

Do you know what your ultimate round would be on your home course? We can tell you, and you'd be surprise at the results! Imagine knowing that you were capable of shooting just 2 over par for your home course instead of your usual 22 over - if only you could string all your best holes together in a single round.

Follow other golfers to see how they are doing, and hopefully get an insight into the competition and the next big event!

##For the golf society or group ##

For golf societies or groups, we go much further, allowing season long as well as weekend and single round competitions to be held. There are leader boards, handicap adjustments for the bandits, team balancing suggestions and more.

Each member can also view their group rounds in amongst their individual rounds, allowing them to monitor their improvements, and watch for those problem holes that always seem to catch them out when on the way to victory!
