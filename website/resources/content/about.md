**Analyse my Golf** was originally built as a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet, tied to a Microsoft Access database way back in 1998 when I played in a local pub group. I used to go out in the first group, and when I got back to the club, I'd get the laptop out and enter all the scores for each group as they finished their round. 

The program then used to calculate all the Stableford scores, front 9, back 9 and overall winners, and make handicap adjustment suggestions for the bandits. It also kept year long scores for everybody, and allowed me to output all the data in static HTML pages for me to upload to my personal site.


Since then the plan has always been to transition the system to a proper web-based service, with crowd-sourced venue and course information, handicap calculations, team / club / society events and match-up suggestions, course ratings, comparisons with your playing peers, etc. 

A recent turn of events, and the facilities that [Amazon Web Services] provides has made the dream a reality.  

One of the most interesting things I have built is the 'course eclectic' where your best holes from all rounds on a particular course are gathered together in a single round. With a handicap of 18, on my home course (par 72), my eclectic was an amazing 58, as at one time or another I had birdied 10 and eagled 2 of the 18 holes!
  
There is still a long way to go, as I can only spend a few evenings a week working on it, but the designs and ideas are all written down, I just need to turn it into a reality! The site is still very much a work in progress, but feel free to register on the free trial to receive more information as and when updates are released.
